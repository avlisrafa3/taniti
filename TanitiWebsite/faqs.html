Frequently Asked Questions

What is the voltage of power outlets on Taniti?

Power outlets on Taniti are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

Are there any restrictions on alcohol consumption?

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.

What is the drinking age on Taniti?

The drinking age on Taniti is 18, but it is not strictly enforced.

Do Tanitians speak English?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. However, very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.

Are there medical facilities on the island?

There is one hospital and several clinics on Taniti. The hospital has many multilingual employees.

Is crime a concern on Taniti?

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, but there are increasing reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes as tourism grows.

How do national holidays affect tourists?

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays. Many tourist attractions and restaurants may be closed on these holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.

What currency is used on Taniti?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency. However, many businesses also accept euros and yen. Currency exchange is available at several banks, and major credit cards are widely accepted.
